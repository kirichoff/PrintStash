# Code of Conduct

PrintStash uses the Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct.

## Our Pledge

We want this project to be a useful, welcoming place for people who self-host,
3D print, maintain printer farms, write integrations, or just want their files
to be less chaotic.

Be respectful. Assume good intent, but listen when someone says a comment or
interaction is not landing well. Keep discussions focused on the work, the
software, and the real-world setups people are trying to support.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, personal attacks, or sustained disruption
- Doxxing or sharing private information
- Sexualized language or imagery in project spaces
- Dismissive behavior toward beginners, non-native English speakers, or people
  running different hardware/software than you

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; block
accounts; or take other reasonable action to keep the project healthy.

Report problems privately to the repository owner through GitHub.
