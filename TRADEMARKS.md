PrintStash name, logo, and branding are not licensed for unrestricted use.

## Allowed Uses

You may use the PrintStash name to:

- Refer truthfully to the original PrintStash project.
- Link to the official repository.
- Describe compatibility with PrintStash.
- Identify an unmodified self-hosted installation of PrintStash.

## Uses That Require Permission

You need prior written permission from the maintainer to use the PrintStash name,
logo, or confusingly similar branding for:

- A hosted, SaaS, or managed service.
- A commercial product or paid service.
- A fork, derivative work, or modified version.
- A domain name, subdomain, social account, package name, app listing, or store
  listing.
- Any use that suggests endorsement by, affiliation with, or operation by the
  original PrintStash project.

## Forks and Derivatives

Forks and derivatives must use a clearly different name and branding unless
permission is granted. They may state that they are "based on PrintStash" or
"compatible with PrintStash" if that statement is accurate and not misleading.

## No Implied Endorsement

Do not imply that a third-party service, fork, integration, or commercial offer
is official, endorsed, or operated by the PrintStash maintainer unless you have
written permission.
